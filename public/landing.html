About iExplore

iExplore provides the best Education experience

Here at iExplore our mission is to provide passion, innovation, and service. All of our teachers are responsible teenagers that are passionate about science, engineering, and literature. They share their passion for learning as well as their knowledge to provide a global perspective for students around the world.

Video made by Daniel Chen

Passion

All of our teachers are passionate in STEM and humanity subjects. They devote their time and energy for students to have a top-notch learning experience.

Innovation

Our teachers encourage their students to harness creativity and develop critical thinking skills allowing them to take their ideas to new heights.

Service.

Through teaching and sharing their knowledge, our iExplore teachers are giving back and reinvesting in their community by helping students expand their visions.

Webinars

Besides small groups, iExplore also hosts webinars which everyone is welcomed to join. These webinars are taught by a teacher who specializes in a certain topic. Our webinar topics are not limited to academic subjects. Books and movie discussion are also included.

Learn More

Individualized Group Course

iExplore provides small group discussions for three to five students. These groups are led by a responsible teenage teacher. This is an effective way for the students to have a more inclusive way to discuss.

Learn More

Volunteer

Teenagers who are willing to help others while challenging their leadership and communication skills are welcomed to join iExplore’s teachers community. In this community, teachers from around the world come together, lead small groups or seminars, and grow together.
